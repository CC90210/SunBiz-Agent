# Helios Quickstart — What Can I Ask?

> One-page cheat sheet for Ezra. Helios owns the outreach lane. He drives cold campaigns, revival sequences, and NEPQ-style discovery. Plain English throughout.

---

## Cold outreach campaigns

| What you want | Say to Helios |
|---|---|
| Start a new cold campaign | "Start a cold outreach campaign. The list is [X merchants], construction industry, $30K+ revenue." |
| Check campaign status | "How is the [campaign name] cold campaign doing? Response rate?" |
| Pause a campaign | "Pause the cold campaign. I want to review before the next step fires." |
| Resume a campaign | "Resume the [campaign name] campaign." |
| Add merchants to a campaign | "Add [merchant names] to the current cold campaign." |

## Ghosted-deal revival

| What you want | Say to Helios |
|---|---|
| Find ghosted deals | "Which deals have gone dark in the last 30 days? Show me the list." |
| Start a revival sequence | "Start a revival sequence for the deals that haven't responded in 3+ weeks." |
| Draft a revival message | "Write a revival message for [Name]. They went dark after the first call. MCA consolidation angle." |
| Pattern-interrupt opener | "Give me 3 pattern-interrupt openers for a merchant with 4 MCA positions." |

## Reply triage

| What you want | Say to Helios |
|---|---|
| Classify a reply | "I got this reply: '[text]'. Is this a hot lead, warm lead, or dead?" |
| What to say next | "They replied: '[text]'. What should I say next?" |
| Handle an angry reply | "They replied that they're not interested and asked to stop texting. How do I handle this gracefully and remove them?" |

## A/B testing

| What you want | Say to Helios |
|---|---|

| Generate variants to test | "Give me 3 different opening messages to A/B test for the construction industry list." |

## Compliance reminders

Helios knows these rules but they are worth keeping top of mind:

- **Never say "loan"** — use "funding" or "working capital"
- **CASL compliance** — all SMS sends include opt-out instructions; Helios enforces this automatically
- **No contact before 8am or after 9pm** local time — enforced by the cold outreach runner
- **One opt-out = permanent removal** — Helios removes the number from all future campaigns automatically
- **Cold list vs warm pipeline** — cold leads live in `cold_lead_lists`, not the main pipeline. They graduate to the pipeline only after a qualifying response
