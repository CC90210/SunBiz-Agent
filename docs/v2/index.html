How to use this library

This batch is built as a working outbound campaign, not just standalone flyers. The five Cold Sequence emails (T1–T5) are deliberately plain-text — research shows text-style cold emails get roughly 2–3× the replies of heavy graphics and land in the inbox, not spam. The branded HTML templates below are for warmer, opted-in, or replied-to leads. Send the cold sequence on this cadence:

TouchDayTemplateJob
T1Day 0Cold IntroPersonal hello + the core differentiator (fast, no hard pull)
T2Day 3Value AddOne concrete proof point: the speed timeline
T3Day 7–10Social ProofOne specific funded story, matched to their industry
T4Day 14BumpUltra-short nudge — reply onto the T1 thread
T5Day 21–24BreakupRespectful close-the-loop — pulls a final wave of replies

Personalization tokens (find-and-replace, or auto-fill from the Command Center):

{{first_name}} {{business_name}} {{industry}} {{city}} {{rep_name}} {{rep_title}} {{rep_phone}} {{rep_email}} {{prequal_amount}} {{referral_reward}} {{year}} {{unsubscribe_url}}

Before you send: every “Apply” link currently points to the existing JotForm (form.jotform.com/253155026259254) so previews work. When the Command Center form is live, it’s a single find-and-replace per file to swap it. Factor rates, terms, and qualification numbers on the Tier Offer are illustrative — confirm them with underwriting before sending.
