How to use this library

Every template here is designed and on-brand - dark navy with gold rails, the SunBiz logo up top, and a green Apply button - modeled on the highest-converting funding emails. They’re built mobile-first and inline-styled, so they render the same in Gmail, Outlook, and on a phone. The five Cold Sequence emails (T1-T5) run as a campaign on this cadence:

TouchDayTemplateJob
T1Day 0Cold IntroPersonal hello + the core differentiator (fast, no obligation)
T2Day 3Value AddOne concrete proof point: the speed timeline
T3Day 7-10Social ProofOne specific funded story, matched to their industry
T4Day 14BumpShort nudge - reply onto the T1 thread
T5Day 21-24BreakupRespectful close-the-loop - pulls a final wave of replies

Personalization tokens (find-and-replace, or auto-fill from the Command Center):

{{first_name}}{{business_name}}{{industry}}{{city}} {{rep_name}}{{rep_title}}{{rep_phone}}{{rep_email}} {{prequal_amount}}{{referral_reward}}{{year}}{{unsubscribe_url}}

Before you send: every Apply link currently points to the existing JotForm (form.jotform.com/253155026259254) so previews work - swap it to the Command Center form when it’s live (one find-and-replace per file). Funding figures are illustrative at a 1.25 factor rate; actual amount, term, and payment are confirmed at quote and subject to underwriting approval. And drop in SunBiz’s real postal address (the footer currently shows a placeholder).
