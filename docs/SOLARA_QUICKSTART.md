# Solara Quickstart — What Can I Ask?

> One-page cheat sheet for Ezra. Solara understands plain English. Copy-paste or rephrase freely.

---

## Pipeline & deals

| What you want | Say to Solara |
|---|---|
| Full daily plan | "Give me the full daily plan." |
| Specific deal overview | "Pull up everything on [Merchant Name]." |
| Stuck applications | "Which applications haven't moved in 5+ days?" |
| Move a lead to next stage | "Move [Name] to [stage name]." |
| Add a note to a deal | "Add a note to [Name]: [your note]." |
| Find deals by criteria | "Show me all hot leads from last week." |

## Underwriting

| What you want | Say to Solara |
|---|---|
| Run underwriting | "Run underwriting on [Name]'s application." |
| Get sales angle | "What's the best pitch for [Name]'s situation?" |
| Check readiness score | "What's [Name]'s readiness score? Why?" |
| Re-run with new statements | "Re-run underwriting on [Name] — new bank statements are uploaded." |

## Shop-out

| What you want | Say to Solara |
|---|---|
| Shop out a deal | "Shop out [Name]'s application using our lender list." |
| Check lender responses | "Have any lenders replied on [Name]'s shop-out?" |
| Best lender for a deal | "Which lenders are the best fit for [Name]? Why?" |
| Check offer status | "What offers have come in for [Name]?" |

## Follow-ups & renewals

| What you want | Say to Solara |
|---|---|
| Set a follow-up reminder | "Remind me to follow up with [Name] on [day/date]." |
| Today's follow-ups | "What follow-ups are due today?" |
| Upcoming renewals | "Who is coming up for renewal in the next 30 days?" |
| Draft renewal message | "Draft a renewal outreach for [Name]." |

## Sequences & drip

| What you want | Say to Solara |
|---|---|
| Enroll a lead in a sequence | "Enroll [Name] in the welcome sequence." |
| Check sequence status | "What step is [Name] on in their drip sequence?" |
| Pause a sequence | "Pause the sequence for [Name]." |

## Status & health

| What you want | Say to Solara |
|---|---|
| System health | "Is everything running? Any errors?" |
| Lender overnight replies | "Did any lenders reply overnight?" |
| New leads today | "What leads came in today?" |
| Campaign performance | "How is the cold outreach campaign doing?" |

---

## Teaching Solara

| You want to | Say |
|---|---|
| Correct her | "That's not right. [correction]" |
| Set a rule | "Remember: [rule]. Always apply this." |
| Confirm a good answer | "That's right. Do it like that going forward." |
| Stop a behavior | "Stop doing [X]." |

## Dashboard shortcuts

- `/t/sun/` — your main SunBiz Command Center
- `/t/sun/pipeline` — lead pipeline view
- `/t/sun/applications` — application tracker
- `/t/sun/shopping-out` — lender shop-out panel
- `/t/sun/automations` — daemon health and cron jobs
- `/t/sun/sequences` — drip sequence management
